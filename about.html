About Iran Bitumen Supply

Who We Are

Iran Bitumen Supply is a specialized exporter of high-quality Iranian bitumen, serving infrastructure, road construction, and industrial projects worldwide. With deep market knowledge and refinery partnerships, we ensure stable supply, competitive pricing, and consistent quality.

Our Mission

At Iran Bitumen Supply, our mission is to simplify international bitumen procurement by delivering certified Iranian bitumen products, transparent pricing, and reliable export logistics to global markets. With several years of proven experience in bitumen exports, we have established a strong reputation for consistency, compliance, and customer trust, serving contractors, distributors, and trading companies worldwide. By combining technical expertise, complete export documentation (ISO, SGS, and safety datasheets), and responsive customer service, we make procurement faster, safer, and more efficient.
